About me

Hello!

Welcome to my little postcard corner! I'm Alexandra. I've been living in the Netherlands for seven years already, after moving from Moscow, Russia for work. I love playing video games, learning Dutch, travelling (bringing piles of postcards from wherever I go), and spending time with my husband and our two adorable Papillion dogs. One of them, Chantal, shares my profile pic with me. I worked in game development for many years, but at the moment I'm taking a break.

Wishlist

You can check out my collection of received postcards here: https://eu.zonerama.com/amarantha/1451481

Some things I'm into:

  • Transportation: trains (I am crazy about Streamliners! And a bit less crazy about steam trains), train stations, subways, airplanes, trams, funiculars, etc.
  • Art: modern art, avant-garde, impressionism, pointillism, industrial design, primitivism, cubism, rayonism. I am somewhat less into contemporary and classic art, but I am always curious about your local, less-known artists.
  • Early or mid 20th-century architecture: art deco, modernism, brutalism.
  • Food: illustrated food, recipes, regional specialties. I don't really cook, but I love to explore.
  • Urban illustrations: streets, buildings, city scenes, cafes, bookshops
  • Eurovision 2025 - it's my soft spot!
  • Abandoned places: especially bus stops and other artifacts of human life. The more dystopian, the better.
  • Industrial sites. Steelworks, huge mining excavators. If it looks mindblowing, it would be awesome!
  • Art homages and parodies
  • Cold War, soviet dystopia, nuclear arms race, and vintage propaganda pieces.
  • Troubled Birds
  • Maps - Especially metro/public transport maps
  • Road signs
  • Lantern Press (except "Protect Our Parks" - I have those)
  • LouPaper States (not only from their state of origin; I have AL/AR/CA/CO/OH/IL/VA/WI, and on the way RI/AK/HI/MI)
